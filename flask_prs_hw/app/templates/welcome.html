To play the game put your name and your choice of paper, rock or scissors into the address bar after the localhost:5000

eg
localhost:5000/Joe/Rock


Follow this with your opponents name and their choice.

eg
localhost:5000/Joe/Rock/Sue/Paper

hit return and the page will tell you who won!


You can also play against the computer!

The computer players are named:
"HAL-9000", "Deep-Thought", "TARDIS", "Data", "ZX-81", "Robbie-Robot", and "Unbeatable"

To play against a computer, put CPU-Playername/CPU into the player 2 position. To play against a random computer opponent, use the name "CPU".

eg
localhost:5000/Joe/Rock/Data/CPU localhost:5000/Joe/Rock/CPU/CPU
Each computer player has their own play style! Try strategy!
