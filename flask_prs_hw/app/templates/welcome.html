To play the game put your name and your choice of paper, rock, scissors, lizard or spock into the address bar after the localhost:5000

e.g.
localhost:5000/joe/rock


Follow this with your opponents name and their choice.

e.g .
localhost:5000/joe/rock/sue/paper

hit return and the page will tell you who won!


You can also play against the computer!

The computer players are named:
"Hal", "Morag", "Chris", "Hannah", "Zsolt", "Malcolm", and "Harrison"

To play against a computer, put their name into the player 2 position. To play against a random computer opponent, use the name "cpu".

e.g.
localhost:5000/joe/rock/malcolm
localhost:5000/joe/rock/cpu
